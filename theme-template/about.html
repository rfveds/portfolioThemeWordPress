I am a member of the Polish Art Association. For several years I was an art teacher in Poland, conducting art workshops on various techniques for children and adults. Important inspiration for me is nature but my artworks are not exactly realistic. I try to convey what I see, what I remember from places where I was and reflect the atmosphere of the place. According to the words one eye sees the one feels Poul Klee
